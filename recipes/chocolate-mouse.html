Chocolate Mousse

Description

A classic chocolate mousse made the proper French way, as served in fine dining restaurants.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 125g dark chocolate
  • 10g unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 3 tbsp caster sugar

Steps

  1. Beat egg whites and sugar until foamy.
  2. Soft peaks - it should be foamy but have soft peaks that flop at the top.
  3. Whip cream.
  4. Fold together cream and egg yolks.
  5. Fold in melted chocolate.
  6. Fold egg whites into chocolate mixture.
  7. Spoon into individual pots or a larger dish and chill until firm.
